Mizuno Pro™ Series ®

Features

  • Limited Edition, Pro & Pro Select Styles
  • Finest Quality Materials, Handcrafted for Excellence
  • Designs & Pockets Tailored for Every Position

Learn More

The Mizuno Pro series features the highest quality leather and raw materials from around the globe. Designed by master craftsman, the Pro series is available in three designs. Pro Series Limited Edition gloves feature the finest cuts of European leather and are handcrafted in Haga, Japan. Regarded as the finest ball glove leather available, each LE glove comes with a manufacturer reconditioning program that offers a one-time opportunity to restore it to its original condition. Pro Series gloves are handcrafted with a perfectly preformed pocket based on the needs of each position. They are designed with US Kip leather that offers a soft, firm feel that holds up at the highest levels of play. Pro Select Series gloves are made with US Steerhide leather for a firm, durable feel that forms to the hand once broken in.
